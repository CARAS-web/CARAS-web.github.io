Contaminated Land

The peak industry body representing the remediation industry, CRC Care (Cooperative Research Centre for Contamination Assessment and Remediation of the Environment) estimates there are 160,000 sites with contamination issues located across Australia and that approximately 1,000 of these sites are cleaned up each year in Australia.

These contaminated sites are made up of of a variety of different contaminated media including groundwater, surface water, sediment or soil which are often hidden from view because they are buried below ground or covered over with topsoil, fill material or buildings.

Contamiantion may not be discovered until the ground is disturbed by an excavation of the land for a utility installation, development works or maintenance works. Other trigger events may be a sale or transfer of title, the planning process and change of land use, visible pollution or seepage events, due diligence or the like.

Whether it is groundwater, surface water, sediment or soil remeidation that is required, CARAS helps clients navigate the mine field that is a remediation project. Our highly experienceed team of contmaianted land proffessionals offer practical and pragmitic advice taoilored to the specific requirements of the client and the project.

CARAS reviews histroical reports and liasies with environtmenal consultant, Site Auditrs and the EPA to provide independent advice to ensure the client recieves value for money in the chosen remedation technique.

CARAS is one of Australia's leading client side project management consultancies for copntamianted ladn projects.
