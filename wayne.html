Wayne Wilson

Procurment Advisor

Wayne Wilson has been working in the Civil Engineering and Contaminated Land Remediation Industries for 40 years.

Wayne’s expertise is in assessing the risk profile on a civil or remediation project to ensure that all construction risks are identified. Wayne develops a dollar estimate of the risks on a project from first principles based on his extensive experience.

His experience includes business development, management of the estimating department, risk reviews, contract negotiations, development of a proprietary estimating software, management of external consultants and subcontractors.
